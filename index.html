Hi! I'm Andrei

I'm an aspiring developer looking to kickstart my career in web development.

I recently came across "Founders and Coders" and by reading the curriculum and the online reviews I was very impressed and confident that by taking this bootcamp I will gain the necessary skills to get into the industry.

I've always been interested in web technologies, but took a deeper dive into developement just recently when I bought a few online courses on the subject. My intention was to see if I would enjoy persuing a career in this field and I can honestly say I've made the right chioce by studying web development.

The tech stack taught in the course(which happens to be my stack of choice), as well as the inclusive approach "Founders and Coders" takes when it comes to learning, convinced me even more that this is the right course for me.
